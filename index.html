Cowork in Downtown Fairbanks!

The HIVE is a casual coworking space in the historic Lathrop building, simply designed with relaxation and focus in mind. Get into the zone and get deep work done.

Ideally located

Walking distance from the courthouse, post office, parking garage, Marriott and the beautiful Golden Heart Plaza right on the Chena River. And if you're hungry or thirsty, the options are many:

  • Venue: grab coffee in the building, or take in some new art on First Fridays
  • The Crepery
  • Noodle House
  • River City Café
  • Bahn Thai
  • The Fudge Pot
  • Lavelle's Bristro and Taphouse
  • Goldie's
  • Midnight Mine
With other fun attractions nearby, including:
  • Escape Room AK
  • Fairbanks Ice Museum
  • Midnight Sun Festival
  • Yukon Quest building
  • Morris Thompson Cultural Center

The HIVE is operated by Andrew McKnight, a software engineer who moved to Fairbanks in the summer of 2018 and fell in love with the place. Working remotely, he split time between his dry cabin and various cafés before deciding he needed some work space of his own. For the HIVE, he brings experience from tech companies in Boston and San Francisco, and hopes to combine them with interior Alaskan sensibilities.
